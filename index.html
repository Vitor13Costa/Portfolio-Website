BuildIT

BuildIT is an intuitive web app, for both desktop and phones, that lets users upload their CAD 3D models or blueprints and then automatically pulls live pricing from major retailers like Home Depot and Lowe's to find the best cost-per-volume for the user's project. Users can assign different materials to specific sections of their design and get real-time pricing. This project was build by a team of 7 students, I was in charge of the Backend development, using languages like SQL and JavaScript. BuiltIt empowers both professional and hobby enginners to spend less time researching costs and more time building.
